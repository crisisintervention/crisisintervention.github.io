Written by mental health 07-05-2014 importance reviews (0) Mental health is related to reasoning, emotion, and behavior in different situations of everyday life. It also helps to determine how to deal with stress, live together with other people and make important decisions. As well as other forms of health, mental health is important at all stages of life, from childhood and adolescence to adulthood. The deficit in mental health could contribute to many somatic and emotional illnesses such as depression anxiety. The issue of mental health, also does not concern only the aspects of care to the emergence of mental disorders, but which corresponds also to the field of the prevention of them with the promotion of a socio-cultural environment determined by issues such as self-esteem, interpersonal relationships and other elements that should already come from the most elementary of children and youth. It is the State of balance between a person and their socio-cultural environment that guarantees their participation labor, intellectual and relations to achieve a well-being and quality of life. Mental health is not just the absence of mental disorders. It is defined as a State of well-being in which the individual is aware of his own abilities, can cope with the normal stresses of life, can work productively and fruitful and is able to make a contribution to their community. It is related to the promotion of the well-being, the prevention of mental disorders and the treatment and rehabilitation of persons affected by these disorders.

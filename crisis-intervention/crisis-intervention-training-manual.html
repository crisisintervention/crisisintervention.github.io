SEPTEMBER 2010 The address Specialized outpatient: provides medical care ambulatory and specialized in geriatrics, cardiology, dermatology, otolaryngology, physical medicine and rehabilitation, Gynecology and traumatology. Acute hospitalization: provides 24-hours-a-day hospitalization for older adults who suffer from acute and chronic processes reagudizados. It features working for hospitalization of these patients. Hospital's geriatric day (HODIGE): it is a service of daytime functioning, no hospital beds, to complete the physical, psychological, functional and social recovery of the adult patient with functional grade I to III (patient who can mobilize and carries out its activities only requires a little help). It serves as an intermediate step between hospital care and return home. The patient goes some days of the week to the HODIGE finally possible functional recovery, continue with the hospital study on an outpatient basis, to complete the therapeutic control. Thus attempting to avoid income and readmissions, extended hospital care. It also offers specialized social assistance. In short, is an intermediate step with large number of benefits in geriatric care. Geriatric home care (ADOGE): This service makes home visits to patients who are enrolled once per month and monitors the complications of patients with functional grade IV and V (patient bedridden who can not move), both from the medical point of view, as social nursing and ensure a continuity of care at home training to family caregivers always looking for the coordination with the emergency service of the HG PNP SJ. Physical medicine and rehabilitation: offers physical therapy and rehabilitation procedures aimed at the restoration of patients presenting any deficiency physical, mental, sensory disabilities temporary permanent. Care is provided in an outpatient and inpatient. Emergency: It provides care 24 hours a day, offering immediate and permanent treatment adult patients in emergency situation. Patients who require more complex care are referred to the PNP LNS HN". This service includes beds of observation and critical care (critical areas).
